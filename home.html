Academics

In line with the vision of our Honourable Chancellor to raise Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences to international standards, CBCS has been introduced from the academic year 2013-2014 onwards for the UG programme. The system is now introduced to PG programme also.
CBCS offers more flexibility to the students to earn credits across departments with wider choice outside their discipline of their study and enables flexible learning within a structured model.
